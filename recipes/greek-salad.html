Greek Salad

Description

The Greek Salad is extremely popular in the world and if you haven't tried it, it's a perfect chance to do it now.

So, how to make a good Greek Salad?

Ingredients

  • Iceberg lettuce
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Onions
  • Feta cheese
  • Italian dressing

Steps

  1. Cut the lettuce and put it into the bowl
  2. Cut the cherry tomatoes and cucumber and add it to the salad
  3. Chopped the onions, add it as well
  4. Shred the feta and add it on top of the salad
  5. Finally, lightly add some italian dressing on top

Enjoy the dish!
